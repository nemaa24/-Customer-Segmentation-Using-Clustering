# ZARA Customer Segmentation using K-Means Clustering

### Project Overview
This project performs **Customer Segmentation for ZARA** using **K-Means clustering** on 20,252 product records.  
We analyzed purchasing behavior through price, sales volume, description length, materials, seasonality, product placement, and promotion response — revealing **4 clear customer personas**.

**Goal**: Help ZARA deliver **personalized marketing**, optimize promotions, improve inventory placement, and increase customer loyalty & ROI.

---

### Business Objectives
- Segment customers based on behavior and purchasing patterns  
- Understand characteristics of each group  
- Generate actionable business insights  
- Recommend tailored marketing strategies  
- Deliver final report with stunning visualizations

---

### Project Workflow (Week-wise)

| Week       |  Task                                                                                                                                         |
|------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Week 1** | Data Loading, Cleaning & EDA <br> Handle missing values, duplicates, and outliers <br> Feature engineering (`desc_length`, `price_category`) |
| **Week 2** | K-Means Clustering <br> Optimal K=4 (Elbow + Silhouette) <br> PCA for 2D visualization                                                       |
| **Week 3** | Cluster Profiling & Insights <br> Named segments with clear behavioral traits                                                                |
| **Week 4** | Final Report & Marketing Strategy <br> Professional visuals + Power BI dashboard                                                             |

---

### Final Customer Segments

| Segment                  | Key Traits                                      | Behavioral Insight                  | Recommended Strategy                              | Priority Channel                     |
|--------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------|
| **Everyday Essentials**  | Low price, very high volume, short descriptions | Budget-conscious, high-frequency    | Flash sales, bundles, heavy discounts             | Mass email + Instagram Reels         |
| **Core Fashion**         | Medium price & volume, balanced seasons         | Reliable all-year shoppers          | Influencer collabs, mid-season campaigns         | Instagram & TikTok                   |
| **Seasonal Trending**    | High promotion response, seasonal focus         | Trend-driven, impulse buyers        | Limited-time offers, countdowns, urgency banners  | Website banners + Email              |
| **Premium Statement**    | High price, long descriptions, premium materials| Luxury seekers, brand loyal         | VIP early access, storytelling, lookbooks         | Exclusive newsletter + In-store events |

---

### Key Insights
- Premium customers spend more but rarely use promotions → perfect for **loyalty programs**
- Deal-sensitive shoppers dominate volume → maximize ROI with **targeted discounts**
- Moderate spenders can be upsold with **personalized recommendations**

**Business Impact**  
Personalized Marketing  
Higher Campaign ROI  
Better Inventory Planning  
Improved Customer Retention
